# Research Paper Analysis Prompt

Analyze this research paper in accessible terms. Assume the reader is intelligent but not an expert in this specific field.

Provide:

## 1. Plain English Summary
What did they do and why does it matter? Explain like I'm a smart friend, not a specialist.

## 2. Key Findings
What are the main results? List in bullet points.

## 3. Methodology (Simplified)
How did they conduct the research? What data or experiments did they use?

## 4. Limitations
What are the caveats? What can't we conclude from this study?

## 5. Practical Implications
How could this research be applied in the real world?

## 6. Related Work
What papers or topics should I read next to go deeper?

## 7. Technical Details (Optional)
For readers who want more depth: key equations, algorithms, or technical contributions.

---
Format the output as a clean markdown report.
