# Article Analysis Prompt

Analyze this article/blog post.

Provide:

## 1. Summary (2-3 paragraphs)
What is the core argument? What conclusions does the author reach?

## 2. Key Points
List the main ideas in bullet form. Be specific.

## 3. Evidence & Data
What statistics, research, or examples does the author cite to support their argument?

## 4. Author's Perspective
What bias or viewpoint does the author have? What's their background or motivation?

## 5. My Takeaways
What's worth remembering from this article? What ideas are actionable?

## 6. Critiques (if any)
Are there gaps in the argument? Counterpoints worth considering?

---
Format the output as a clean markdown report.
