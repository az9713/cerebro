# Podcast Episode Analysis Prompt

Analyze this podcast episode transcript thoroughly. Extract maximum value from this conversational content format.

## 1. Episode Overview
- **Podcast Name**: What show is this from?
- **Episode Title**: Episode name
- **Host(s)**: Who hosts this podcast?
- **Guest(s)**: Who is being interviewed or featured?
- **Episode Length**: Duration
- **Core Topic**: What is this episode about?
- **Episode Type**: Interview, solo, panel, narrative, etc.

## 2. Comprehensive Summary
Provide 3-4 paragraphs covering:
- The main topic and why it matters
- Key discussion points and flow
- Major insights and revelations
- Conclusions or key takeaways

## 3. All Key Points
Extract EVERY significant insight and claim:
- Main topics discussed
- Key arguments made
- Important stories shared
- Recommendations given
Be exhaustive - capture everything valuable.

## 4. Guest/Speaker Insights
If featuring guests:
- Their background and expertise
- Unique perspectives they bring
- Key stories from their experience
- Advice they share
- Their current projects or work

## 5. Facts, Statistics & Data
Extract all specific data mentioned:
- Numbers, percentages, metrics
- Dates, timelines, milestones
- Research studies referenced
- Industry data shared
- Comparisons and benchmarks

## 6. Stories & Examples
Document narratives shared:
- Personal anecdotes
- Case studies discussed
- Origin stories
- Failure and success stories
- Hypothetical scenarios

## 7. Frameworks & Mental Models
Capture conceptual tools introduced:
- Named frameworks or models
- Decision-making processes
- Ways of thinking about problems
- Categories or typologies
- Memorable analogies

## 8. People, Companies & Resources
- Individuals mentioned
- Companies and organizations
- Books recommended
- Tools and products
- Other podcasts or content suggested

## 9. Notable Quotes
10-15 memorable moments:
- Quotable insights
- Funny or surprising statements
- Contrarian viewpoints
- Well-articulated ideas
Include approximate timestamps if available.

## 10. Actionable Takeaways
What to DO with this information:
- Immediate actions to take
- Ideas to implement
- Experiments to try
- Things to research further
- Habits or practices to adopt

## 11. Questions Raised
- Topics that warrant deeper exploration
- Unanswered questions from discussion
- Counterarguments to consider
- Gaps in the conversation

## 12. Latent Signals
Surface insights that are implied but not explicitly stated. Only include genuine inferences - do NOT fabricate signals.
- **Unstated assumptions**: What do the speakers take for granted?
- **Implied predictions**: What future trends are suggested?
- **Hidden dynamics**: What's the subtext of this conversation?
- **Industry signals**: What does this reveal about the space?
- **Audience signals**: Who is this really for and why?
- **Contrarian indicators**: What's NOT being discussed?

## 13. Connections
- Related episodes to find
- Other podcasts on similar topics
- Books or articles mentioned
- People to follow
- Topics to explore further

---
Format the output as a clean markdown report.
