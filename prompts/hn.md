# Hacker News Post Analysis Prompt

Analyze this Hacker News post and discussion thoroughly. Extract value from both the linked content and the community discussion.

## 1. Post Overview
- **Title**: The post title
- **Link**: URL being discussed (if any)
- **Author**: Who submitted this?
- **Points**: Score and popularity
- **Post Type**: Link, Show HN, Ask HN, Launch HN, etc.
- **Core Topic**: What is being discussed?

## 2. Linked Content Summary
If this links to an article, paper, or project:
- 2-3 paragraph summary of the linked content
- Main thesis or purpose
- Key claims or features
- Why this is newsworthy or interesting

## 3. Discussion Highlights
Analyze the comment section:
- **Top-level themes**: What are the main discussion threads?
- **Consensus views**: What do most commenters agree on?
- **Controversial points**: Where do opinions diverge?
- **Expert insights**: Any particularly informed comments?

## 4. Key Insights from Comments
Extract the most valuable contributions:
- Technical clarifications or corrections
- Personal experiences shared
- Additional context provided
- Alternative perspectives offered
- Resources and links shared by commenters

## 5. Facts & Data Shared
From both post and comments:
- Statistics and numbers mentioned
- Technical specifications
- Performance benchmarks
- Cost/pricing information
- Timeline or version information

## 6. Technical Details
If applicable:
- Technologies or tools discussed
- Implementation details
- Architecture decisions
- Code snippets or examples
- Technical trade-offs debated

## 7. People, Companies & Projects
- Individuals mentioned (founders, developers, experts)
- Companies and startups
- Open source projects
- Products and services
- Related or competing solutions

## 8. Community Sentiment
How does HN feel about this?
- Overall reception (positive, negative, mixed)
- Common praises
- Common criticisms
- Skepticism or concerns raised
- Excitement or interest shown

## 9. Notable Quotes
5-10 memorable comments or passages:
- Insightful observations
- Funny or witty remarks
- Expert explanations
- Contrarian viewpoints

## 10. Actionable Takeaways
What to DO with this information:
- Things to try or build
- Tools to evaluate
- Concepts to learn more about
- Approaches to consider

## 11. Critical Analysis
- What's the signal vs noise ratio?
- Are the criticisms valid?
- What context is missing?
- How reliable is the discussion?

## 12. Latent Signals
Surface insights that are implied but not explicitly stated. Only include genuine inferences - do NOT fabricate signals.
- **Market signals**: What does this post suggest about industry trends?
- **Technology trajectory**: Where is this space heading?
- **Community priorities**: What does HN engagement reveal?
- **Hiring/talent signals**: What skills are in demand?
- **Startup opportunities**: What gaps or needs are mentioned?
- **Contrarian indicators**: What popular views might be wrong?

## 13. Follow-up Resources
- Links shared in comments
- Related HN discussions to find
- Projects to explore
- People to follow
- Topics to research

---
Format the output as a clean markdown report.
